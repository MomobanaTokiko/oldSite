About

Japanese Creator VTuber who spread their wings everywhere from the game world to the real world.


Whimsical VTuber who loves games　Tokiko Momobana

桃花Momobana 鴇子Tokiko is a VTuber who loves games, mainly distributing games and chatting. Despite her bright, kind, and cute voice, she has gained a strong fan base from the mysterious comments she sometimes makes.

She is a VTuber, but she is also active in a variety of other fields, such as drawing her own illustrations and writing her own novels. She is currently active as a VTuber while writing her first novel, “ネトゲNet game廃人Haijin予備軍Yobigun,” which is her representative work.
